Enterprenour

Be Your Own Boss: You set your own schedule, rules, and direction, removing the need to answer to a superior.

Freedom to Create: It allows you to transform your innovative ideas into a tangible product or service.

Unlimited Potential: Income and rewards are not capped by a salary; high risk often brings higher potential for financial gain.

Passion-Driven Purpose: It is often driven by a need to solve problems and make a personal, positive impact.

Constant Growth: You learn, develop, and adapt rapidly, turning every mistake into a valuable lesson.

High Risk, High Reward: It is a "rollercoaster" of emotions; the path is often lonely and requires intense dedication (sometimes 65+ hours a week).

Mindset Over Career: It is more of a mindset or psychological predisposition than just a job.

Immediate Action: True entrepreneurs do not wait for the perfect time; they "start before they are ready"
